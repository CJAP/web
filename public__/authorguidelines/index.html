Submission Guidelines

Blog posts must be submitted as a word document (.doc or .docx) Written in 12pt Times New Roman on 8 1⁄2” x 11” page Margins cannot be smaller than 1.9cm on all sides All submissions must include a photograph or image that fits with the content of the post (see copyright section for more information on images) Maximum of 12 references (in APA format) must accompany the submission

  1. Blog post: Think about what the take-home message is from your research, how this fits within current literature and why this topic is important and/or interesting. You should also make sure to include a good hook, your thesis statement and 3 pieces of evidence for your thesis statement in your blog post.

  2. Ownership & Licensing document: Please read, print, sign and attach this document to your submission email along with the blog post and signatures document. If you have any questions about this document please email us at : journalofaccessiblepsych@gmail.com.

  3. Signatures document: Please read, print, sign and have your supervisor sign this document. Then send it via email along with the submission and ownership documents. If you have any questions about this document please email us at : journalofaccessiblepsych@gmail.com.

  4. Photo to accompany your article: All blog posts need to be accompanied by a photograph or image which should reflect the main argument in the blog post.

If you take the photograph, make sure all individuals in the photo have given consent to be photographed and are aware these photos will be posted on-line to accompany your blog post. If you wish to retrieve an on-line image, please use website that allow for the free download of images such as unsplash (https://unsplash.com/). This photo/image should be included in the blog post submission but also as a separate file.
